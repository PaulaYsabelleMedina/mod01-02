How to make overnight oats

Ingredients

  • Chia seeds
  • flax seeds
  • oats
    • Rolled oats
    • crushed oats
  • water

Instruction

  1. Mix chia seeds, flax seeds and oats
  2. Put water
  3. Let soak overnight

Food Info

Chia seeds
Chia is the edible seed of Salvia hispanica, a flowering plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae) native to Central America, or of the related Salvia columbariae of the southwestern United States and Mexico. Chia seeds are oval and gray with black and white spots, having a diameter around 1 millimetre (0.04 in).
Flax seeds
Flax (Linum usitatissimum), also known as common flax or linseed, is a member of the genus Linum in the family Linaceae. It is a food and fiber crop cultivated in cooler regions of the world. ... In addition to referring to the plant itself, the word "flax" may refer to the unspun fibers of the flax plant.
